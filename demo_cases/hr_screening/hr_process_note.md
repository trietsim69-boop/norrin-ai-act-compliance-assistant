# Internal HR Process Note — Candidate Screening Pilot

**Department:** People Operations  
**Process owner:** Maria Chen, Head of Talent Acquisition

## Current workflow

1. Applicants submit CVs through our careers portal.
2. TalentRank AI ingests applications nightly and assigns a ranking score.
3. Recruiters review the **top 20 ranked candidates** each morning.
4. Recruiters may override the ranking, add notes, or reject candidates before interview invitations are sent.

## Human oversight

- No candidate is rejected automatically without recruiter review in the pilot phase.
- Final interview decisions remain with the hiring manager.
- Recruiters can disable AI ranking for specific requisitions (e.g. executive hires).

## Open questions

- Whether the organisation is **provider or deployer** of the ranking model (vendor hosts the model; we configure job profiles).
- Whether rejected candidates receive any explanation tied to the AI score.
