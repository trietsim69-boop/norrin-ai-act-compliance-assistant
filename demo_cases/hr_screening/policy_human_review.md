# HR Policy — AI-Assisted Hiring (Draft)

**Status:** Internal draft — not yet approved by Legal

## Scope

This policy covers use of third-party AI tools in **recruitment, candidate screening, and applicant ranking**.

## Principles

1. AI outputs are **recommendations only**; recruiters retain decision authority.
2. All ranked shortlists must be reviewed by a qualified recruiter before candidates proceed.
3. Protected characteristics must not be used as model inputs (gender, ethnicity, age, disability status).
4. Applicants must be informed that AI assists the screening process (transparency notice in progress).

## Provider / deployer

Norrin HR Tech develops and hosts the TalentRank model. Our organisation configures job profiles and operates the system as a **deployer** in EU hiring processes.

## Risk note

Legal has flagged that employment-related AI may fall under **Annex III high-risk** categories. Full conformity assessment not yet completed.
