# MoodSense Analytics — Vendor Pitch

**Vendor:** EmotiLab Inc.

MoodSense is a workplace analytics platform that infers **employee mood and emotional state** from webcam video and voice patterns during remote meetings and at desk checkpoints.

## Value proposition

- Real-time **emotion recognition** dashboards for team leads.
- Alerts when aggregate team mood drops (burnout early warning).
- Integration with HRIS for wellness programme targeting.

## Signals analysed

- Facial expression analysis (micro-expressions).
- Voice tone and speech pace.
- Optional self-reported mood check-ins (combined with inferred affect).

## Deployment context

Designed for **workplace** and hybrid-office environments. Pilots running with three enterprise HR departments in the EU.
