# Workplace Deployment Note — MoodSense Pilot

**Site:** Berlin HQ + remote engineering teams  
**Sponsor:** Workplace Experience

## Rollout plan

- Phase 1: Opt-in pilot with 120 **employees** on voluntary basis.
- Cameras analyse facial cues during stand-ups (with consent banner).
- Managers receive weekly mood trend reports aggregated at team level.

## What is inferred

The vendor documentation states the system infers **emotional states** such as stress, engagement, frustration, and positive affect. It does not claim to diagnose clinical conditions.

## Employee concerns

Works council has asked whether this constitutes prohibited **emotion recognition in the workplace** under the EU AI Act. No legal opinion received yet.

## Exceptions under discussion

- Could safety-critical monitoring (e.g. lone-worker alert) qualify for an exception?
- Medical use is not planned.
